Who am I?

My name is João Paulo, and I am a master's student in Pharmaceutical Sciences at the Federal University of Juiz de Fora - UFJF. As an academic, I will share a bit about "why did I choose pharmaceutical sciences?" Well, I like to think that it was the pharmaceutical sciences that chose me, perhaps it is due to the scientific curiosity I have had since childhood.

A curiosity that motivated me to learn about small things, like atoms, for example; though they are so small, they are grandiose when seen from a different perspective. Or perhaps the reason goes far beyond my enthusiasm and fascination in wanting to discover and understand the chemical and biological experiments that are part of life.

The lessons that my teachers have stimulated me to understand - to not falter at the first difficulty and to forsake change, make it worth every sleepless night, that I have had and will have ahead, so that “I,” the astute scientist, can face risks with great responsibilities. And consequently, I can have valuable new experiences in this pharmaceutical universe. A universe filled with possibilities that only benefits those who are part of it.
